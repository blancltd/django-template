Paragraphs

A paragraph (from the Greek paragraphos, “to write beside” or “written beside”) is a self-contained unit of a discourse in writing dealing with a particular point or idea. A paragraph consists of one or more sentences. Though not required by the syntax of any language, paragraphs are usually an expected part of formal writing, used to organize longer prose.

Blockquotes

A block quotation (also known as a long quotation or extract) is a quotation in a written document, that is set off from the main text as a paragraph, or block of text.
It is typically distinguished visually using indentation and a different typeface or smaller size quotation. It may or may not include a citation, usually placed at the bottom.

The LinkButtonBlock (named Button in admin blocks) allows buttons to be added, linked to either an internal page, custom url, or download link. The button can also be styled by primary and secondary colour, or outline styles. The LinkBlock should be extended for links on other blocks - example seen on the ImageBlock. This is the block used for adding videos and other embeddable objects like iframes

Two Column Block

Block displaying a full width intro section, and a left and right column of content. Styles can be added to the block, giving it a background colour, or changing the alignment of the left and right columns.

Colour options: Primary, Secondary, Grey and None. Alignment options: Equal 50:50, Wide left column 60:40, Wide right column 40:60, Wider left column 70:30, Wider right column 30:70.
